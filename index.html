The essentials

Registration & must-dos

The things that keep you working — verified against the official sources.

🎓
Care Certificate — now 16 standards (learning disability & autism added 2025). The CQC-expected baseline for everyone new to care.
🩺
NMC registration & revalidation — nurses revalidate every 3 years. The Revalidation Companion builds the whole pack for you.
🔒
DBS — care roles need an Enhanced check with barred-list check. The £16/yr Update Service makes it portable.
🏛️
The bodies that affect you — NMC, CQC, RCN, NICE, Skills for Care. Who they are & why they matter is in Trusted Resources.
📍
Your nation matters — Scotland, Wales & NI require personal registration (SSSC / Social Care Wales / NISCC). England doesn't.
