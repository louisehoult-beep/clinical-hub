NHS pay bands (Agenda for Change)

England, 2026/27, full-time annual. Changes every April — always check the live figure before relying on it.

BandTypical role
2Healthcare / care assistant
3Senior HCA / support worker
4Nursing Associate / Assistant Practitioner
5Registered Nurse (newly qualified)
6–7Senior / specialist nurse, ward manager, ANP
8+Matron / advanced practice / lead nurse

Scotland, Wales and Northern Ireland run separate pay scales — the table above is England only.
